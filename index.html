Fomalhaut: The Lonely Star of Autumn Skies

As fall evenings turn cooler and unfamiliar star patterns come into view, Fomalhaut appears low in the southern sky. Often called the Lonely Star of Autumn, it glows with a pale blue-white hue, standing apart in a calm, sparsely populated region of the southern heavens.
